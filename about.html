About Eros4NRG

The project is dedicated to transforming the trustworthiness, security, and transparency of energy data ecosystems by leveraging Zero Trust Architectures (ZTA) and explainable AI (xAI). In the evolving landscape of smart grids and digitised energy markets, Eros4NRG introduces an advanced approach to data security, ensuring reliable and traceable energy transactions.

As a plugin for the NEMO Meta-Operating System, Eros4NRG integrates into the NEMO Smart Energy Trial (Smart Energy and Mobility Living Lab), demonstrating its capabilities in real-world energy data management. The project is designed to benefit key stakeholders, including ASM Terni (DSO) and EMOTION (EV fleets), by providing secure, data-driven solutions for energy system optimization.

Technology & Innovation

Eros4NRG applies cutting-edge technologies to secure IoT/Edge data streams, ensuring the integrity and reliability of energy systems. Key technological components include: Zero Trust Architecture (ZTA): A cybersecurity framework that eliminates implicit trust and continuously verifies every access request, enhancing resilience against cyber threats in energy networks. Explainable AI (xAI): AI-driven analytics and machine learning models that provide transparent, interpretable insights into energy data, enabling informed decision-making. Interoperability with the NEMO Meta-Operating System: Ensuring seamless integration within NEMO’s Smart Energy and Mobility Living Lab, fostering collaborative energy data sharing and real-time monitoring. By addressing fundamental challenges such as data authenticity, trust, and interoperability, Eros4NRG empowers energy stakeholders to harness data securely and efficiently.

Project Impact

Eros4NRG aims to create a more secure, transparent and efficient energy ecosystem. Its impact extends across:

  • Energy Sector: Enhancing data integrity in smart grids and decentralized energy markets.
  • Mobility: Supporting electric vehicle (EV) fleet operators with accurate and secure energy data for efficient charging and grid interaction.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Facilitating trustworthy energy transactions aligned with evolving EU cybersecurity and data governance standards.
  • Sustainability: Contributing to the broader digitization of the energy sector, driving efficiency and resilience in smart energy systems.

Project Objectives

Eros4NRG is committed to achieving the following core objectives:

  • Enhance Trust in Energy Data: Develop and integrate Zero Trust Architectures (ZTA) to strengthen security and data authenticity in smart grid environments.
  • Enable Transparent and Explainable AI (xAI): Implement AI-driven insights that offer clear, interpretable decision support for energy stakeholders.
  • Integrate with the NEMO Meta-Operating System: Develop Eros4NRG as a scalable, interoperable plugin to enhance NEMO’s Smart Energy and Mobility Living Lab.
  • Support Energy and Mobility Stakeholders: Provide secure, data-driven decision-making tools for Distribution System Operators (DSOs), EV fleet managers, and energy market actors.
  • Contribute to EU Energy Digitalization Goals: Align with the EU’s data governance and cybersecurity frameworks, driving secure and sustainable energy digitization.
